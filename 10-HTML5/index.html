HTML5 Tags - Video, Audio and Canvas

Embeded Video

The video tag is used to embed video into a webpage. Attributes such as width,height, src, controls, autoplay, loop and muted was used. The video you see below is from the BYUi website and is in mp4 format, which is specified as the value for the "src" attribute. Becuase the "controls" attribute was used, the video controls are available. It will start playing automaticall because of the "autoplay" attribute. The audio will be muted by default because the "muted" attribute was used.

Embeded Audio

The audio tag is used to embed audio into a webpage. Like video, it has many of the same attributes with the exception of width, height and poster. Otherwise, the have the same effect. The mp3 audio clip below is being sourced from the lds.org website and is the choir singing "I am a Child of God".
